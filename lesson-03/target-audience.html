Persona 1

Middle-aged and elderly people who are active or semi-active

These people are tourists in the area, with higher-than-average income who are just planning to spend a few days in the region.

Persona 2

Residents of the area

These are the residents of the area. They are farmers, who have long lived in the region and need to know the weather before they plan their weekly activities.

Scenarios

  • How many inches of rain in the next three days?
  • How much watering will my crops need?
  • Should I pack a sweater for my camping trip this weekend?
  • How much sunscreen will I need for my fishing trip?
  • Will there be thunderstoms that I need to watch for this week?
