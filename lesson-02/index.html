Design Principles

Lucas Moreira

White Space

Apple

apple.com

  I think Apple is a great example of White space since items in their website have a white background every other item. For example, the first item is white, so the second one will have a different colored background while the third one will go back to white.

Fitt's Law

Google

google.com

  Google is a great example of Fitt's Law because they make the focus of the page (the Search bar) be in a very prominent spot on the page. They also make it full width, so it is easier to reach.

Visual Hierarchy

Tesla

tesla.com

  Tesla is a good example of Visual Hierarchy because they have their car as an attention-grabbing item in the page, while the buttons to order and to check inventory come right below it, bringing the user's attention to the possibility of buying one of their cars.
